WHY CHOOSE
ST. PHILIP SCHOOL

CAREFULLY SELECTED TEACHERS

All of our 40 teaching staff members have been rigorously
vetted and selected for their engagement and on a personal and professional level, as well as and pedagogical expertise.

Teaching Methods

Ours is a school where theory meets practice. Our pupils learn Italian and English through a blend of the Direct and Communicative Learning approach alongside the Cognitive Code Approach.

REMOTE LEARNING MODE

In compliance with anti-Covid legislation, our school has put in place remote learning classes.

CURRICULUM

Our Curriculum is built around each pupil’s needs, as they embark on an exciting personal journey. Academic success and personal growth are aided by the wealth of teaching resources, including interactive whiteboards, tablets, Art lab, ITC lab, Science lab.

UP TO 27 HOURS OF ENGLISH

Each week Pre-School: 3 y/o 16 h, 4 y/o 20h, 5 y/o 27h; Primary: Grades 1st, 2nd 27h, 3rd-4th 25h, 5th 21h; Lower Secondary: up to 19h
